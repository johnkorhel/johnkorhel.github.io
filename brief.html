Seasons change, and people change. Take a journey with SZNS, a solo musician from Denver, Colorado. With inspirations of soul, R&B, and rap, SZNS is like no other. With humble beginnings in EDM and house music, SZNS has broken free from the industry norm. As the vocalist, performer, and producer, SZNS does it all. The Debut SZNS album, Overflow, is set to be released in late 2019. Explore his thoughts, merchandise, and tracklist below.
