Legend TSA

Legend TSA was established in --2012-- with only --10-- members and has only grown since. Today their are over 40 members at the Legend TSA branch. Every year we get new and innovative members eager along with previous members to create projects and win!

Legend went to the TSA Nationals in Nashville Tennessee last year, and while there were a few semifinalists no one from Legend won in the 2015-2016 Nationals.

In Legend TSA we hold most of our meetings in Mrs. Tawzer's room. We have almost weekly meetings for the whole club to meet and work on projects.
